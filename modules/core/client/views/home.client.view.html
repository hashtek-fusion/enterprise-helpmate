The DETS AT&T Business Solutions (ABS) Architecture is part of the bigger DETS Architecture team. We provide advanced technology leadership for key areas of focus. The team makes recommendations to match technologies with solutions for business needs, manages and promotes the use of standards and participates in the technology planning process for future capacity and capabilities.
